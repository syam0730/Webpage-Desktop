Are you looking for a nice place to stay with affordable daily or monthly rates that fits to your budget? You browsed into the exact website. Our KBA Complex is the exact choice by offering quality prices and service. Our KBA complex is away from the hustle and bustle of city and a perfect place to stay away from the crowd.
Reserve your spot at the budget-friendly accommodation at Pathalam. Pathalam is a suburban region of the of Kochi, in the state of Kerala, India. KBA Complex is situated close to Eloor, an Industrial District of Kochi. KBA Complex is at a walkable distance from Pathalam Junction which is having access to places like Aluva, Kalamassery, NH47 and Metro Station (3 km away), Eloor(1.5 km), Edayar Industrial Estate (1 km away). KBA complex is 7 Kms away from both Aluva railway station and Lulu Mall. Info Park is located around 9Kms from here. Ernakulam Medical College is only 8Kms away from our place. It is at a walkable distance from the ESI Hospital, Pathalam. Needless to say, you will get easy access to most of the industrial areas in Pathalam, Edayar and Kalamassery.
Whether you are a working man/bachelor/job seeker/student group/family man/tourist worried looking for a right affordable lodge on a daily/monthly basis, we have rooms for everyone and for every budget. We have single, double, triple and dormitory accommodations. Rooms are suitable for families, corporate individuals, tourists, and students who are looking for hygienic low budget accommodation. We can accommodate marriage parties or any function delegates at a feasible rate. We have tailor made packages for student groups who are on Industrial Training.
